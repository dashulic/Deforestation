About Us

Treesponsible is an non-profit organization dedicated to minimizing deforestation world wide. We believe in educating people, because knowledge is power and when people know what's happening they will have the power to stop it.We have a dedicated team of volunteers working to acheive our goal and we are located in Toronto, Canada. Our Mission

Our mission is to making people believe in the existence of deforestation and of the negative effects it has on our planet.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where all deforestation is natural and where we take only what we need.


What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is clearing Earth's forests on a massive scale, often resulting in damage to the quality of the land.
